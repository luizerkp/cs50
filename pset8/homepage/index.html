Wecome to a Lazy Person's Guide to Traveling

First off, thanks for reading my ramblings, in which I attempt to sell you on traveling....wait... before you click that exit button hear me out for a bit; plus...I got pictures! The poor attempt at humor aside, I would ike to ask; why do we love to travel? There are many equally valid answers to that question but I think it can all be boiled down to one simple answer; "because we get something from it". At this point you may be shaking your head at the screen and saying "well duhh!! why else". Hear me out first then you can shoot me later. Some people travel to put notches on a belt, for example: to visit the 7 Wonders, the most "Exotic", the tell most impressive travel stories. Some do it, to show off to others either via a blog, website or social media. Some just want an escape and most do it for more than one reason. I'm here to tell you that any reason why you travel is valid as long as it brings you something of value be it spiritual, emotional or material…So go forth and travel make, memories and see the world outside your backyard!!
