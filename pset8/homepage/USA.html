Hello, There!!

The United States of America is a melting pot of different peoples and cultures. Immigration from all over the world has overall made this country better and stronger. Composed of 50 states, the USA feels like 50 different countries held together by an unbreakable bond. Each state is filled with deep history and different traditions, so no matter where you go, you will find something to do and learn. This country, which is also my home, has many great adventures and memorable moments to offer. From the beaches and amusement parks in Florida to the majestic view of Lady Liberty in New York to the walk of fame in California, there is bound to be something for everyone. For locals like myself this country also has so much to offer when it comes to adventures and should not be underestimated. As Mentioned before 50 states are a lot to cover so consider this guide as a taste of what this country has on offer. Don't be afraid take the trip or If you are a local get out of your comfort zone and go somewhere you've never been before even if it is just a few hours car drive from your home.

Preparing to visit the US

If you want to visit the United States, you first need a travel visa unless you are a citizen of Canada or Bermuda. To find out more visit the State Department website for the most up to date information on visa requirements, application process and processing times. I recommend ensuring your passport does not expire within 6 months and to check the state department visa appointment wait times page as processing times can vary between localities. If you are a US citizen or resident then visiting the United States is as simple as waking up in your bed so nothing else is required other than an adventurous spirit.

Planning the Trip:

The first thing you will want to do is to buy a airline ticket to your desired destination or as simple as filling you gas tank if you are local. Note that it can be cheaper to travel to SDQ and then take a transfer over to your final destination. I have found Skyscanner to be very useful in getting the best deals on airfare, Otherwise you can try the other big names like Expedia or Priceline.
